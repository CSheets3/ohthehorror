Plot:
I usually don't like Wild West movies, because they're slow and drag the plot. This movie was quite different. It does have some slow development, but it sure delivers. This movie is absolutely brutal.
Plot Rating: 9 / 10


Characters:
Kurt Russell and Patrick Wilson are legends. That's all that needs to be said.
Character Rating: 8 / 10


VFX:
I didn't really notice anything out of place. Some of the gory parts were wild and I appreciate that.
VFX Rating: 8 / 10


Setting:
Like I said earlier, Wild West movies aren't really my thing, but the setting of this movie is so...unsettling. It was dirty, gritty and raw. Defintely the best part about this movie.
Setting Rating: 8 / 10


Scoring:
There's a serious lack of scoring in this movie which is why it felt slow. To be fair it did give you a sense of how alone you were when you were traveling back in these days.
Scoring Rating: 7 / 10


Final Rating: 8.0

Solid movie. If you like Kurt Russell you will love this movie!
