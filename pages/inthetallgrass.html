Plot:
The plot was really interesting. Although, nothing everything was explained the way it should have been. There was a few plot holes that left me confused. Overall, it wasn't executed very well.
Plot Rating: 5 / 10


Characters:
The characters were pretty bland, nobody really had a personality and they were just there fill a role. There wasn't any clear line of thinking which is mildly understandable given the situation, but it could have been better.
Character Rating: 4 / 10


VFX:
Nothing wrong with the VFX. Some of the grass was a little out of place, but nothing harsh.
VFX Rating: 8 / 10


Setting:
I really did enjoy the setting to this movie. It takes place in a pasture, isolated from society. The fact that they can make this setting fit the plot was a major plus.
Setting Rating: 8 / 10


Scoring:
The scoring was okay. There are a few chants to go along with it which really brings out the darkness in the movie.
Scoring Rating: 8 / 10


Final Rating: 6.6

The production value was great, but the movie fell short of my expectations.
If you like Stephen King then you may enjoy this movie more.
