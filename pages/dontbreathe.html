Plot:
I appreciate the newer idea it brought to horror, but I thought the plot could have been more detailed. There was a minor twist, but nothing that will throw you out of your seat.
Plot Rating: 6 / 10


Characters:
Having 3 main characters really works for this movie because it did allow for some development. These 3 people come from different backgrounds and really expands on why they're robbing someone.
Character Rating: 7 / 10


VFX:
Nothing too much. More of a movie that doesn't rely on VFX; which is good.
VFX Rating: 7 / 10


Setting:
The setting was plain but very effective. It's a robbery so it's expected to take place in a house.
Setting Rating: 8 / 10


Scoring:
This was my favorite part of the movie really. The way the plot is told, the movie needs to be quiet. The lack of scoring really helps you feel anxious in this movie.
Scoring Rating: 7 / 10


Final Rating: 7.0

This movie wasn't anything special. It was made well, with a cool idea, but didn't meet my expectations.
