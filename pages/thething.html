Plot:
For being made in 1982, The Thing was amazing. It had a solid plot that places the characters in an isolated locating leaving them to deal with the events head on.
Plot Rating: 8 / 10


Characters:
Overall, the characters were pretty good. There are a couple characters that are fillers, but everyone seems to have their place.
Character Rating: 7 / 10


VFX:
This movie had fantastic FX. They were practical, and really gave the antagonist some life. Defintely made the movie that much better.
VFX Rating: 10 / 10


Setting:
A movie with this kind of plot and a setting taking place in Antartica really made this movie shine. I really liked how Antartica made them isolated from society and you got a sense of lonliness because of it.
Setting Rating: 9 / 10


Scoring:
The scoring was pretty generic, but it didn't get in the way with the rest of the movie.
Scoring Rating: 7 / 10


Final Rating: 8.2

For being made in 1982 this movie was wild. It's an instant classic a must see!
