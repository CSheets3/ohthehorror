Plot:
This movie brings a fresh take on demon-horror. I wasn't expecting some of the twists and it's executed quite well.
Plot Rating: 8 / 10


Characters:
The characters were well developed. This dark of a movie with a touch of comedy some of the characters bring mixed very well. The way that these charactes behave makes the situation very believeable and interesting.
Character Rating: 8 / 10


VFX:
Some of the scenes and characters were detailed very well and nothing looked outrageous. Very pleasing to the eyes.
VFX Rating: 8 / 10


Setting:
It takes place in the present day with a regular family. Later you learn that something sinister is taking place. Between the creepy house and The Further, it will leave you with goosebumps throughout the whole movie.
Setting Rating: 9 / 10


Scoring:
The movie was rather quiet and made you feel alone. It worked very well and when the scoring came in, it will fill you with dread and uneasiness.
Scoring Rating: 10 / 10


Final Rating: 8.6

This is one of the only movies that made it hard to sleep at night. Definately worth the watch!
