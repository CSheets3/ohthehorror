Plot:
Krampus is a great Christamas horror movie to watch! It's dark but has some comedic relief that really pulls this plot together.
Plot Rating: 8 / 10


Characters:
Characters were funny and well put together. Not everyone played a huge part in this movie, but everyone added value to the story.
Character Rating: 8 / 10


VFX:
Great. They had some fun moments with the monsters and such.
VFX Rating: 7 / 10


Setting:
You never really expect anything horrifying to happen on Christmas, but this movie performed well. The setting was light and happy while the plot was dark and sinister.
Setting Rating: 9 / 10


Scoring:
Nothing to say about this section.

Final Rating: 8.0

Krampus was a very fun movie to watch. Brought something fresh to the table and had fun with it!
