Plot:
Very interesting plot and well developed. I didn't really expect anything great from this movie, but it was really fun to watch!
Plot Rating: 8 / 10


Characters:
The characters were medicore. This movie was at its best when the action scenes occured. The only real form of development was the cliche good guy vs. bad guy situation. Nothing special.
Character Rating: 6 / 10


VFX:
It did have some pretty sweet VFX added. I'd say they're more realistic many of the movies now-a-days.
VFX Rating: 8 / 10


Setting:
I actualy enjoyed the setting. WW2 is very interesting to me and this movie seemed to capture more of the 'behind the scenes' point of view during the war.
Setting Rating: 8 / 10


Scoring:
Pretty generic, nothing special. Just kept the flow of movie going.
Scoring Rating: 7 / 10


Final Rating: 7.4

Overlord was a refreshing movie to watch. It brought in some cool ideas and it's worth the watch.
