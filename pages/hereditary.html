Plot:
WOW. That's all I have to say. This movie was absolutely wild. The plot was so developed and left you hanging until the very end. The way the movie flows is just amazing. It's a slow burner but stunning!
Plot Rating: 10 / 10


Characters:
This movie has it all. These characters were so convincing! Everyone was so realistic and the plot really gave these characters life. The acting was phenomenal.
Character Rating: 9 / 10


VFX:
This movie really didn't need VFX, but it was so-so.
VFX Rating: 7 / 10


Setting:
The setting...fantastic. This whole movie is creppy from start to finish. Esepecially the treehouse. All the props were set up so well and really drove the plot to completion.
Setting Rating: 10 / 10


Scoring:
The movie really didn't need scoring either. It was rather quiet and worked very well that way. Really gave you a small space feel.
Scoring Rating: 9 / 10


Final Rating: 9.0

I'll be damned if this movie isn't an instant classic! Watch it immediately!
