Plot:
While the plot to The Witch is fairly simple, the execution is amazing. It takes place in New England in the 1630's. A family moves away from a small civilization due to religious conflicts. While they are living alone, strange events unfold that leads them to believe they are not alone.
Plot Rating: 7 / 10


Characters:
The characters were actually well developed. As the movie continues they clash and start questioning each other's faith in order to explain what's happening to them. No characters were fillers and I believe they made rational decisions given the situation and time period.
Character Rating: 7 / 10


VFX:
Not too much to say about this category. Everything looked well, the camerawork was great and there weren't any overdone parts.
VFX Rating: 9 / 10


Setting:
As mentioned before, it takes place in New England in the 1630's. The location was truly amazing as it gave the audience a visual to how isolated they were from the rest of society.
Setting Rating: 9 / 10


Scoring:
The scoring was amazing. There wasn't too much noise in the background and it makes you feel claustrophobic; making you feel more immersed into the setting. Overall, it was a quiet movie, which really helped with the tension.
Scoring Rating: 9 / 10


Final Rating: 8.2

I strongly recommend watching this movie!


Side Note: I thought this movie was very well done, but you really have to be in the mood to watch it because it is easy to fall behind.
