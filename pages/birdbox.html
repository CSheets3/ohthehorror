Plot:
Very interesting plot. I thought it was well executed, but there were a few plot holes that were left unexplained. There was almost too much openess within it. Although it was a fresh take on horror and it performed well.
Plot Rating: 8 / 10


Characters:
Everyone but the main characters were only okay. There wasn't really much development and it only seemed to revovle around one person. Which is fine, but there were too many characters to take that route.
Character Rating: 6 / 10


VFX:
Nothing really stood out as I don't think this movie needed that much. It was primarily used in the background gave the film a better setting.
VFX Rating: 7 / 10


Setting:
The setting was alright. They had some cool locations, like rafting down a river. Overall it fit well with the plot.
Setting Rating: 8 / 10


Scoring:
Pretty generic, nothing special. Just kept the flow of movie going.
Scoring Rating: 7 / 10


Final Rating: 7.2

This movie really wasn't suprising, but it wasn't that bad either. If you have the time I would watch it.
