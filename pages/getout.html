Plot:
The plot really blew me away. It was incredibly detailed with a lot of setups and payoffs. It takes place in the present day within a private community. This movie will keep you on the edge of your seat!
Plot Rating: 10 / 10


Characters:
Each character had so much personality. It was filled with realistic dialogue and humor and they will leave you with goosebumps throughout the whole movie.
Character Rating: 9 / 10


VFX:
There really wasn't any space for extra VFX. Which is great because then it feels authentic.
VFX Rating: 9 / 10


Setting:
It takes place in a wealthy, seculded neighborhood and you really feel the isolation as the movie continues rolling. This was a great place for the plot to develop.
Setting Rating: 9 / 10


Scoring:
The scoring was little out of the ordinary, but the movie was a different experience all together. The scoring definately set the tone for creepiness.
Scoring Rating: 9 / 10


Final Rating: 9.2

This movie was outstanding! The more you watch it, the more details you pick up on!
