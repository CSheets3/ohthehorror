Plot:
This movie was electrifying! The plot is really the same as other zombie movies, but they way they deliver it...fantastic!
Plot Rating: 9 / 10


Characters:
The characters were well carved out actually. At first I thought there was going to be too many characters, but they dwindled it down pretty quickly.
Character Rating: 7 / 10


VFX:
Nothing too much, the zombies looked great though.
VFX Rating: 6 / 10


Setting:
This movie takes place everywhere. On a train, in the city for a moment. Really adventurous. The way they used to train was interesting too.
Setting Rating: 8 / 10


Scoring:
Pretty generic. No complaints.
Scoring Rating: 7 / 10


Final Rating: 7.4

Overall, it was a very exciting movie to watch! Certainly passed my expecations!
